Interpersonal Innovation

Collaboration is a crucial aspect of invention, when multiple people work together with different skills to make something new.

Why does it help?

The purpose for having people together for innovation is relatively intuitive. If people are able to easily meet someone who could help solve a proble, that process becomes quicker. The work culture becomes closer and allows for people to collaborate with all sorts of people.

Limitations

However, there are some downsides to this. Remote work became commonplace due to the pandemic, which changed the culture of even a typical office. Employees could work from their couch, or make a small office of their own. One downside for the open layout for innovation was a pressure to always be productive, working longer hours and burning out. Hybrid work, with intentional collaboration may be the new future (Miller).
